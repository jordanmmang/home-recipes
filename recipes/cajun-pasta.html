Spicy Cajun Pasta

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken sausage links, sliced
  • 1 carton baby bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cups dry penne pasta
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp. tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. Cajun seasoning (see recipe)
  • 1/2 tsp. chicken bouillon
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the directions on the package. Drain, rinse and set aside to cool.
  2. In a large sauté pan, heat 1 tbsp. olive oil over medium heat. Cook the sausage and mushrooms together. If using pre-cooked sausage, make sure it’s heated through thoroughly. If using raw sausage, make sure it's cooked thoroughly. Remove from the heat and set aside.
  3. In a large sauté pan or pot, heat 1 tbsp. olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and cook 4-5 minutes, or until they begin to turn translucent. Add the garlic and cook another 30 seconds, or until fragrant.⁣
  4. Add the chicken bouillon to the chicken stock and vinegar mix and stir until dissolved. Add this mixture to the pan, reducing the heat to medium-low. Allow to simmer until half of the liquid has dissolved, roughly 5 minutes.
  5. Add the tomato sauce, Cajun seasoning, and smoked paprika. Stir until dissolved.
  6. Add the heavy cream and bring back up to a simmer. Allow to simmer until some of the liquid has evaporated and the sauce has thickened, roughly 8-10 minutes.
  7. In a large container, combine the cooked pasta, cooked chicken sausage and mushrooms, and pasta sauce. If needed, season with salt and pepper to taste.
