  • 2 tsp. paprika
  • 1 1/4 tsp. oregano
  • 1 1/4 tsp. thyme
  • 1 tsp. onion powder
  • 1 tsp. cayenne powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. In a bowl, stir together the garlic powder, paprika, oregano, thyme, onion powder, cayenne powder, salt, smoked paprika, black pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Store in a sealed jar in a cool, dry place.
