Taco Seasoning

Ingredients

Serving Size:

  • 1 tsp. chili powder⁣⁣
  • 1/2 tsp. paprika
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. cumin
  • 1/4 tsp.cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt⁣⁣

Directions

  1. In a bowl, stir together the chili powder, paprika, garlic powder, cumin, cayenne pepper, onion powder, and salt⁣⁣.
  2. Store in a sealed jar in a cool, dry place.
